Citi Bike Dockblocking

One way to rate the reliability of a bikeshare is to compute the probability of stations being full. We used data collected from Citi Bike’s live station GBSF feed at 10-minute intervals, giving frequent “snapshots” of dock status between April and October 2016 (7 months). The data is shared on the public Google group CitiBikeNYC Hackers. Each record shows the available dock count for every station for a total of 17.8 million records.



Lower East Side

Number of stations: 20

Dockblocking: evening peak / night

One of the most vibrant neighborhoods in Manhattan, the LES experiences heavy dockblocking after 9 pm. The probability that a station is full rises to 25% after 9 pm and climbs to an average of 31% between 9 pm and 2 am



Financial District - South Street

Number of stations: 5

Dockblocking: morning peak

At the core of the downtown criscrossed by Wall Street and Water Street, this cluster of stations is primarily affected during the morning peak between 9am and noon . The probability of these stations being full during this time period is an average of 27%



East Village

Number of stations: 13

Dockblocking: evening peak / night

This neighborhood experiences the worst dockblocking between the hours of 7 pm and 2 am , with the average probability of finding a full station 22%
